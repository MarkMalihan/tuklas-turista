Brgy. Calimpak, Guinayangan, Quezon

MSPL was pronounced a protected area on April 23, 2000 by virtue of Proclamation No. 295. This 149-hectare protected area encompasses different barangays such as Himbubulo, San Pedro I, and Magsaysay. The MSPL is not just a tourist attraction but also the primary source of water for the municipality of Guinayangan. The protected area has hosted various biodiversity and biological research and has also gained some popularity as an eco-tourism destination. Facilities currently offered at MSPL are bamboo cottages, adult and children’s water founding facilities with crystal clear running water (man-made lagoons), waterfalls, a clean restroom, a view deck, canopy walk, and an eco-trail. MSPL is just a stone’s throw away from the municipal public market.
